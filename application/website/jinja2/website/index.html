ABOUT US

Cuisines have their own blend, their own aroma and they leave their own imprint in history. We, Indians are the flavour lovers and we like our food to be rich with aroma and right amount of spices. Biryani, just mentioning the word is enough to arouse and excite the gastronomic juices of million plates.

We understand the value that a good entrée of delightful food holds. 3 years back with a single vision to serve you only the best with authentic taste and uncompromising quality, “Petu Ki Biryani” was founded on April 2015. We established Petu Ki Biryani only to produce good cuisine, impart excellent services and provide a delightful and consistent experience to our food lovers.
