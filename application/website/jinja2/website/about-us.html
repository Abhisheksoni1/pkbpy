ABOUT US

Cuisines have their own blend, aroma and they leave their own imprint in history. We, Indians are the flavour lovers and we like our food to be rich with aroma and right amount of spices. Biryani, just mentioning the word is enough to arouse and excite the gastronomic juices of million plates.

We understand the value that a good entrée of delightful food holds. 3 years back with a single vision to serve you only the best with authentic taste and uncompromising quality, “Petu Ki Biryani” was founded on April 2015. We established Petu Ki Biryani only to produce good cuisine, impart excellent services and provide a delightful and consistent experience to our food lovers. At Petu Ki Biryani, we focus on a meticulously crafted culinary adventure, that not only tantalizes your taste buds but will take your heart away. One of the things that make us unique among our customers is that we pitch very high with our food, sourcing freshest ingredients, carefully sourced meat and high-quality cuisines while keeping the prices reasonable for our customers.

To ensure that we live upto the customer’s satisfaction and the above philosophy are our elements such as time-tested recipes, fresh and premium ingredients be it spices, mints, herbs, our professional staff, innovative practices and intense commitment towards the food and food lovers.

PkB’s Special Handmade Handi Biryani

Besides giving you the delicious tender pieces of meat swaddled in flavours, and perfect fluffy grains of rice, each a microcosm of everything flavourful, goes into the handi. The handi allows the steam to tenderise the meat in its own juices retaining the original taste of the rice and other contents in it, while keeping bacterias at bay.
